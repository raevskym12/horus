Horus uses topological data to map the distribution of plant species throughout the rainforest.

Horus Intel enables you to identify and target land that yields the highest profits when sutainably harvested for medicinal and nutritional plants of interest in your industry.


Solution




The average acre of rainforest has an expected lifetime yield of $460 if used for timbering and agriculture, or $2,400 if used for sustainable harvesting of edible, medicinal, and other commercially valuable plants.



Our statistical models identify the most likely contents of rainforest areas so land developers can best utilize each plot of land.

Impact

25% of all prescription drugs and over 70% of all cancer treatment medications originate from the rainforest. However, less than 1% of all rainforest plant species have ever been studied.

As we lose 34 million acres of rainforest per year, we also lose a gold mine of medicine. Our data mitigates environmental destruction by optimizing land usage.
